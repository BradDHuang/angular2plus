Directives:

Attribute Directives vs. Structural Directives

Attribute D.
    Look like a normal HTML Attribute
    Only affect / change the ele. they are added to

Structural D.
    Look like a normal HTML Attribute
    but have a leading *
    Affect a whole area in the DOM
